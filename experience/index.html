The Living System

What is a
living system?

Not a pipeline — an ecosystem with a flame at its core.

If you love something, make it your career. Inspiration, intuition and innovation aren't slogans here — they're the gravity that holds every part of MCTC in orbit.

The system sustains itself: students train, get placed, and return to lift the next batch. No goodbye on graduation day — only a different role in the same family.

Public deadlines make the work real — the Creative Technology Event at Junction City has run every year since 2017, alongside HOGCON, festivals and brand collabs.

Train

Theory, tested
by deadline.

Study and practical work, fused into one track.

MCTC is Pearson UK BTEC Centre No. 92672 — your diploma is a real UK credential, from Level 3 Foundation up to HND (Level 5), with a Top-up Bachelor pathway in development.

BTEC framework with real production embedded inside it — 120 CATS credits at HND, 240 on the full bachelor pathway, aligned to global standards.

Mentors who ship real work — client briefs from Ooredoo, Nestlé and MSI run inside the classroom, and mistakes are corrected in production, not punished by exams.

Place

No certificate-
and-goodbye.

Every graduate is routed somewhere real.

You graduate with shipped work, not just grades — exhibition pieces, client projects and a public showcase that art directors actually look at.

The in-house studio takes commercial work — EDM visuals, AR filters, game art for Nestlé, MSI, Wacom, Bandai, NVIDIA. Earn while you teach, teach while you earn.

The coalition network places graduates directly into studios, agencies and ateliers — and mentorship doesn't end at the door.

75% of current faculty walked in as students first. The strongest graduates are invited back to teach — the loop that keeps the system alive.
